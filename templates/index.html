yabata — Singapore Bus Arrival Timings

Yet Another Bus Arrival Timing Application. Real-time bus arrival estimates for every bus stop in Singapore, powered by the Land Transport Authority (LTA) DataMall API.

Add your frequently used bus services, organise them into groups, and see when your next three buses are arriving — all on one screen. Use the Nearby feature to find bus stops around you with live arrival information for all services at each stop.

  • Real-time arrival timings from LTA DataMall
  • Nearby bus stop finder with geolocation
  • Shortcut groups — organise by route, stop, or destination
  • Drag-and-drop reordering
  • Cross-device sync with a memorable phrase
  • Light and dark theme
  • Works on any device — installable as a PWA

Supports all bus operators in Singapore: SBS Transit, SMRT, Tower Transit, and Go-Ahead Singapore.
